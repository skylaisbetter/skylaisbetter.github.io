Artifact #2

These are weird faced people that I made. The faces started off as a square but I morphed the right side to my liking and copied it to the left side, then I morphed the top and copied it to the bottom. By doing so I made a shape that fits perfectly with itself. I then made a face and added color and this was the finished result.
